Ad hoc Reporting

A number of views support ad hoc reporting. You will know if a view supports ad hoc reporting when the buttons for generating html and PDF light up from to in the toolbar. The first button will generate the range of selections into html, the second will generate into PDF.

A number of pre built filters have been added into the Entry Accounts screen to make selection of a number of entries easier. Once the entries are selected, they will form the basis of the ad hoc reports.
